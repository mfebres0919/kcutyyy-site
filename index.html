First Visit?

Welcome,
New Client

Everything you need to know before your first appointment — so you can walk in and walk out looking fresh.

01
What to Expect

Your first appointment is a full experience. We start with a quick consultation about your hair goals and the style you want. From there, I handle everything — cut, fade, lineup, and finish — so you leave looking clean and fresh.

02
Come With Clean Hair

Please arrive with clean hair — no heavy products or buildup. Clean hair allows for the best fade lines, sharper edges, and a cleaner overall cut.

03
Bring Your Inspo

Have your inspiration photos or reference cuts ready. Whether it's a specific fade style, cut style, or lineup — knowing what you want helps me deliver exactly what you're envisioning.

04
Plan Your Time

A typical haircut takes 30–90 minutes depending on the service. Longer services like full cuts with beards may take more time. Don't rush — great cuts take time. Kick back and bring headphones if you like.

Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked

How long does a haircut take?+

Most haircuts take 30–60 minutes. Full cut + beard services may take 90 minutes or more. You'll receive a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to book in advance?+

Yes — we strongly recommend booking in advance. Walk-ins are welcome when available, but appointments are guaranteed. Book online or DM us on Instagram to lock in your slot.

What styles do you specialize in?+

We specialize in skin fades, taper fades, bald fades, lineups, edge-ups, and beard work. Whether you want a razor-clean bald fade, a high-top, or a fresh taper — we've got you covered.

How often should I come in?+

For fades and tapers, we recommend coming in every 2–3 weeks to keep it looking fresh. The tighter you like your cut, the more often you'll want to come back.

Is a deposit required to book?+

Yes — a $25 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. This deposit goes toward your total. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid forfeiting your deposit.

Do you do kids' cuts?+

Absolutely! Kids cuts are welcome and priced separately. Just let us know when booking. We love making sure the little ones leave looking sharp too!

Before You Come In

Shop
Policies

A few things to know before your appointment — so we can give you the cleanest cut possible.

01
Deposit Required

A $10 non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking. This goes toward your total balance on the day of your appointment.

02
Cancellations & Reschedules

Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid losing your deposit. Use the link in your confirmation email to make changes.

03
Grace Period

There is a 10-minute grace period for late arrivals. Appointments may be cancelled after this point and the deposit will be forfeited.

04
Come Prepared

Please arrive with your inspiration photos ready and your hair clean with no heavy products. This helps us deliver the best cut possible.

05
No Extra Guests

To maintain a focused shop environment, please keep it to yourself or one guest. Kids getting cuts must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

06
Questions?

If you're unsure about anything before your visit, reach out via the contact form or DM us on Instagram. We're happy to help you prepare.
